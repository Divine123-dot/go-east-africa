About Us

The Go East Africa intend to advertise the captivating tourism destinations in east Africa countries like Burundi, Rwanda, Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda,
as a rising travel destination in Africa by exposing its unique culture, beauty and adventure opportunities.

Mission

Our mission is to draw the international and domestic travellers through a compelling, insightful and user-friendly platform

Our Values and Approach

Our values are rooted in integrity, usability, and sustainability

  • Integrity:We provide unbiased, accurate logistics guides and travel advice, ensuring travelers have reliable information that is not solely driven
    by commercial booking interests.
  • Usability: Our website design is focused on clear navigation, mobile responsiveness, and easy access to core information, making trip planning efficient.
  • Sustainability: We encourage tourism that supports local communities and respects the unique wildlife and cultural heritage of East Africa.

Our story

Go East Africa was founded to support international and domestic travellers by offering accurate travel information. Our aim focus is on East Africa region,
especially Burundi, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda and Tanzania. The Company focuses to guide the tourists who want to explore undiscovered World of Africa.
